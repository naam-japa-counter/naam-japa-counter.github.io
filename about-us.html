About Us

Built with Devotion, for Devotees

We are a small team of spiritual tech enthusiasts from India, making simple digital tools for daily sadhana practice.

Our Mission

Why We Built This

We saw that modern seekers needed a peaceful, distraction-free digital tool for their daily mantra and naam japa practice.

Preserve Tradition

Bring the sacred practice of Japa into modern life — without compromising its essence or simplicity.

Protect Privacy

Your sadhana is personal. We build tools that work fully offline — your data stays only on your device.

Build Consistency

Small steps daily lead to lasting transformation. Our tools are designed to keep you motivated every day.

Founder

Dipu Varma

Founder & Developer — dipuguide

Dipu Varma is the creator of Naam Japa Counter. A developer and devotee at heart, Dipu built this app out of a personal need — a clean, simple, and honest japa counter that respects your spiritual space. No ads. No cloud. No distractions.

Based in Uttar Pradesh, India, Dipu combines a passion for technology with a deep respect for traditional spiritual practice. The goal is simple: make good tools that help people stay connected to their faith.
